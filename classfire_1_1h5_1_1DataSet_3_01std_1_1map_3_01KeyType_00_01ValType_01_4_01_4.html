Detailed Description

template<typename KeyType, typename ValType>
class fire::h5::DataSet< std::map< KeyType, ValType > >

Maps.

Very similar implementation as vectors, just having two columns rather than only one.

Note
We assume the load/save is done sequentially. Similar rational as Vectors
